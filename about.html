James Josephson

Originally from San Francisco, James currently lives in Portland, Oregon. He designs for both the web and print, and specializes in front-end development. At On Point Design, James iterates throughout the design proccess to actualize justified and creative ideas.

Leonardo cilmi

Leonardo Cilmi is an award winning graphic designer and artist based in San Francisco. He specializes in web design, user experience, branding, print works, and general graphic design. Leonardo enjoys working with the OPD team and clients in order to bring their ideas to life in the form of elegant and effective end products.
